About Guru Nanak Dev Engineering College

GNDEC, Ludhiana, established in 1956 by the Nankana Sahib Education Trust, is one of the oldest engineering institutions in Punjab. Founded in the name of Guru Nanak Dev Ji, the college was created to provide technical education to the underprivileged. It is affiliated with I.K. Gujral Punjab Technical University and enjoys autonomous status by UGC.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality technical education, foster innovation and leadership, and contribute to nation-building through skilled engineers.
