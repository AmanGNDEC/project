Welcome to Our College


Guru Nanak Dev Engineering College (GNDEC) was established in 1956 by the Nankana Sahib Education Trust. It was founded to provide technical education to underprivileged and rural students in Punjab. The college is named after Guru Nanak Dev Ji, the first Sikh Guru. It is one of the oldest and most prestigious engineering colleges in northern India. GNDEC was granted autonomous status by UGC and has been affiliated with Punjab Technical University.
